Indicator Joint consultative, employment forum or similar
Description

The percentages of positive responses to four questions in ESENER 2019 are displayed for each Member State in a box as percentages. This indicator asks specifically about the existence of:

  • A joint consultative committee
  • Trade union representation
  • A health and safety representative
  • A health and safety committee

Fifteen countries can be displayed at the same time, as well as the EU average.
Data source ESENER 2019 Survey
Specific table/Question

The ESENER 2019 Survey Questions are:

Which of the following forms of employee representation do you have in this establishment? Specifically this indicator anwers to:

  • A joint consultative committee, employee forum or equivalent body
  • A recognised trade union representation
  • A health and safety representative or representative of employee safety
  • A health and safety representative committee

Answer options:
1. Yes
2. No

Additional comments

A description of the social dialogue in each country, downloadable as a PDF file, is available too. The source of the descriptions of social dialogue is a preparatory report for DG Employment, Social Affairs and Inclusion from 2018. Most of the data were compiled in 2016 or 2017.
(Kooperationsstelle Hamburg IFE/EUROGIP: Development and design of a structural model for the construction and implementation of an EU OSH Information System, Final Report, March 2018, Service contract No VC/2016/0055)
